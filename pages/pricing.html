Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose between plans?
↓
Essential is ideal for small businesses needing occasional legal support. Professional is perfect for growing companies with regular legal needs. Enterprise offers fully customized solutions for large organizations with complex requirements. We recommend the Professional plan as it offers the best value and flexibility for most clients.
Can I switch plans anytime?
↓
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the beginning of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll prorate your charges. If you downgrade, credits will be applied to your account.
What payment methods do you accept?
↓
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and invoicing for annual payments. Enterprise clients can arrange custom payment terms. All payments are processed securely through industry-standard encryption.
Is there a minimum contract term?
↓
No. Monthly plans have no minimum commitment and can be cancelled anytime with 30 days notice. Annual plans offer a 20% discount but require a one-year commitment. Enterprise clients work with us on custom terms based on their specific needs.
Do you offer a free trial?
↓
Yes! All Professional plan customers get a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start. After the trial, you can continue with a monthly subscription or annual plan. We also offer a custom trial for Enterprise clients.
